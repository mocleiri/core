About

Jmx panel, to view and operate the applications mbeans

How to use

to use put this lines on application pom:
        <dependency>
            <groupId>org.wicketstuff</groupId>
            <artifactId>mbeanview</artifactId>
            <version>1.4-SNAPSHOT</version>
        </dependency>

or download the jar form:

http://wicketstuff.org/maven/repository/org/wicketstuff/mbeanview/

SVN

https://wicket-stuff.svn.sourceforge.net/svnroot/wicket-stuff/trunk/wicketstuff-core/mbeanview-parent/mbeanview
